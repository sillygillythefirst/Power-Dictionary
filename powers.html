INFORMATION

[Usage]:

Powers become second nature to users after some training, the power acts like a part of the user's body. There is also a limit to how much the user can use their power. The more training the user does, the longer they can use their power. ALL DOWN SIDES HAVE CONDITIONS TO HAPPEN. They aren't always given, those are just the effects of using power carelessly.

[Training]:

Powers have a large amount of potential, base powers with less rating can be improved easily. And powers that have a higher rating can also be improved, albeit very slowly. Emotion plays an important part in how strong a user is with their powers. The more control the user has over their emotions the more powerful and controlled the power will be. The less control a user has over their emotions, could be the difference between an outburst that could cause massive destruction and the user's death or power instability.

[Limits]:

A User can only have 2 powers or 2 combined powers (and one passive power), these powers can be opposites but they won't be able to use them at the same time. Powers that aren't opposites will give said user the ability to combine them. For example, if you want Modernity and Nature, since both of these are natural enemies, you can't use them in the same vessel. While Fire and Water can coexist, but using them at the same time will either damage the user or make said powers do less or no damage. Power combination will make those powers slightly less powerful but will allow for up to 4 powers not including passive powers!
